International Student Trends in Australia

This study aims to observe the number of International Students
in Australia. The aim is to provide a clear breakdown the
study sectors (Visa Type) and key geographical regions

Total Number of International Students in Each State(2005-2024)

Number of International Students in Each State/Territory

The number of students in Australia seemed to have increased
over time, but there was a sharp decline in 2020-2021.
which can be justified by the COVID pandemic. Another observations
that is seen, is that Victoria and New South Wales
constantly have the highest number of international students across
the years.
